Instructions

  1. Click on the Tools menu.
  2. When the new window appears, click on the Advanced tab.
  3. Scroll down in the list until you find "Display Accelerator Button on Selection."
  4. Uncheck this option.
  5. Click OK to save the new settings.
  6. Reload the page, and click on the Neahttadigisánit Reader bookmark again.
